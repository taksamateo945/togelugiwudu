He was born on June 7, 1975. Allen Ezail Iverson is the ex-husband of Tawanna Iverson. Moreover, Allen used to be a professional basketball player.

Tawanna and Allen Ezail Iverson met each other in high school. They soon began to date each other. Then, at the age of sixteen, Tawanna gave birth to their first child Tiaura.

Divorce

Tawanna had three more children, all daughters with Allen. Unfortunately, after a decade of marriage, Tawanna separated from her husband.

Due to allegedly physical abuse, Tawanna and Allen separated in 2010. On many occasions, Allen had kicked his wife out of the house due to some arguments.

Moreover, Tawanna had to sell her jewelry to pay many debts. She paid for their kids’ tuition fees all by herself. Tawanna was all alone in this marriage.

While proceeding with the divorce, Allen was struggling financially. As per some sources, he did not even have money to eat.

Luckily, Allen received $32 million due to his contract with Reebok and thus tried to use this money to get his wife back. In addition, he even signed a postnuptial agreement. 

The agreement stated that Allen was not allowed to cause any physical or verbal abuse to Tawanna and be transparent about his money. Unfortunately, Allen’s habits did not change, so Tawanna received USD 32 million.

As soon as the divorce got finalized in 2013, Tawanna gave Allen half of the $32 million since he struggled financially. Moreover, after only a month of divorce, the couple got back together and is currently dating.

During the NBA Hall of Fame, Iverson mentioned his wife as his number one person who always had his back. Together with their five children, Turner was all present at this ceremony.

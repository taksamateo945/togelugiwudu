Leigh-Anne continues solo domination with sultry 'Forbidden Fruit'

Publish date: 2024-03-26

RnB goddess Leigh-Anne is back with the latest installment from her Hard Feelings EP, a sultry offering called ‘Forbidden Fruit’.

Produced by Albert Hype, ‘Forbidden Fruit’ was written by Leigh-Anne alongside J Kash and Nija, and is based on the star’s real-life romance with husband André Gray. The reggae-tinged track shows yet another side to her artistry and follows on from the release of ‘Stealin’ Love’.

Speaking about the track, the former Little Mix star said: “I wrote ‘Forbidden Fruit’ at a writing camp in Miami, and it’s actually about how André and I met. This one gives me sweaty, sultry, reggae dance vibes. The way the beat drops makes me so excited to bring this one to the stage.”

The track comes accompanied by a sensual black and white visual featuring the singer and a group of dancers. Check it out below.

‘Forbidden Fruit’ forms part of the Hard Feelings EP, with more tracks to be unveiled over the coming weeks. These are separate to her debut album which is also in the works.

Since launching her solo career with ‘Don’t Say Love’ and ‘My Love’, Leigh-Anne has cemented her status as one to watch. With each release, she has really come into her own as an artist, dropping irresistible tracks which are unapologetically herself.

Leigh-Anne will be showcasing these tracks and more at her one night only gig at London’s Lafayette on June 11.

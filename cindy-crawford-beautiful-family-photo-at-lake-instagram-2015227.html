Cindy Crawford Shares Beautiful Family Photo at the Lake: See It Here!

Publish date: 2024-03-28

Well, would you look at these beauts! If Instagram is to be believed (and of course it is!), Cindy Crawford is having quite the lovely lakeside family vacation, So naturally, she'd want to share a snap of the whole group—including husband Rande Gerber, son Presley, and daughter Kaia. Serious question: Could this quartet be more stunning?

In the photo, shared on Tuesday, July 21, from what appears to be the Gerber Point Lodge in Canada, Crawford, 49, can be seen smiling while wearing a snakeskin swimsuit (partially hidden underneath a cozy towel). Kaia, 13, clad in a striped T-shirt, nuzzles into her shoulder on one side. Her son, Presley, 16, sits sandwiched between her and her husband while working swim trunks and a slightly shy grin. Of course Gerber, 53, rounds out the group on the far left, looking handsome in a T-shirt and shorts. Crawford's caption? A simple heart.

(We know, it's almost overwhelming!)

Earlier that day, Crawford posted another shot—this one featuring just her budding model daughter. Using the lake as a backdrop, the brunette teen beauty does her best Blue Steel while pulling at a gray sweatshirt that reads—what else?—"Cindy Crawford." "When your daughter steals your clothes…," the international supermodel captioned the pic.

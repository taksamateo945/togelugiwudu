Arsene Wenger and Roy Hodgson meet Frank Spencer

Publish date: 2024-05-09

OK, so tonight is the night that we finally get the return of Frank Spencer, and England coach Roy Hodgson and Arsenal FC manager Arsene Wenger will come face to face with the hapless character.

Sport Relief will tonight feature a very special comedy sketch of the 1970’s hit sitcom Some Mothers Do ‘Ave ‘Em, starring Michael Crawford as the accident prone Frank Spencer.

The skit will feature some very exciting big name guest cameo roles in the form of, David Walliams, Andy and Jamie Murray, Sir Paul McCartney, Jenson Button and of course Hodgson and Wenger.

The new mini episode will also see Michele Dotrice reprise her role as Frank’s long suffering wife Betty, and Gemma Arterton will play the couples daughter Jessica.

The comedy skit will see the Murray brothers watch on as Frank Spencer speeds along the Olympic Velodrome track on his infamous roller-blades, which judging by this photo doesn’t exactly go to plan.

You can watch the full sketch by tuning into Sport Relief on BBC One from 7pm tonight (Friday 18th March).

X Factor Movie Night Songs Revealed

Publish date: 2024-05-12

With Movie Week just around the corner, The X Factor has revealed the acts’ choices of songs for tomorrow night’s Live Show! There’s a wide variety of movies set to make an appearance – Elvis Presley’s Jailhouse Rock to Daniel Craig’s Spectre – so it’s sure to be a great show.

Boys:

Ryan Lawrie: Jailhouse Rock – Elvis Presley (Jailhouse Rock)

Matt Terry: Writing’s On The Wall – Sam Smith (Spectre)

Girls:

Emily Middlemas: Must Have Been Love – Roxane (Pretty Woman)

Over 25’s:

Saara Aalto: My Heart Will Go On – Celine Dion (Titanic)

Honey G: It’s Like That/Getting Jiggy With It – Jason Nervins/Will Smith (Keith Lemon: The Movie/Last Days Of Disco)

Groups:

5AM: Try A Little Tenderness – Otis Redding (The Commitments)

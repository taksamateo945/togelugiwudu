Scott Foley Lies Shirtless in Bed With Puppies for Charisma Photos

Publish date: 2024-03-01

Be still, our hearts! Scandal hunk Scott Foley is the star of a sexy new ad campaign, and if these photos don’t make you want to run out and buy whatever he’s selling, nothing will. All you need to know are these five words: Shirtless. In bed. With puppies.

The images, for luxury home brand Charisma, show the 42-year-old Felicity alum in various states of undress as he cuddles tiny, adorable dogs atop a plush white comforter. In some of the pics he wears boxers, while in others he sports drawstring pajama pants — but in all of them he’s shirtless.

Speaking about the shoot in a behind-the-scenes video on set, Foley said he doesn’t think of himself as a typical heartthrob. “I would say that I never feel sexy,” he admitted. “My wife [Marika Dominczyk] has told me that I am sexy. And people on Twitter tell me that I’m sexy. So there must be something there, right?” said the hunk, who split from first wife Jennifer Garner in 2004.

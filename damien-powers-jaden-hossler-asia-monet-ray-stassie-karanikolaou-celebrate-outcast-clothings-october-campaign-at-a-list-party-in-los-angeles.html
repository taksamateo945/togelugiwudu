Damien Powers, Jaden Hossler, Asia Monet Ray &amp; Stassie Karanikolaou Celebrate Outcast Clothings O

Publish date: 2024-04-07

A new season means a fresh wardrobe, and trendy it-girl brand Outcast Clothing has a ton of new, smoking hot looks that are must-haves for your upcoming fall threads.

With sexy cutouts, daring styles and matching sets at the forefront of their latest collection, Outcast celebrated their October campaign with an invite-only cocktail party at The Fleur Room in Los Angeles last Wednesday (October 4) and the Celeb Secrets gals were one of the lucky ones to nab a spot on the guest list.

Good vibes were spread all around the venue, as the gals were dressed to the nines in their best Outcast fits while the guys played it cool in trendy looks that fit the overall theme of the evening. We even rubbed shoulders with celebs like Damien Powers, DDG, Diablo, Dylan Lee, Jacob Sartorius, Jaden Hossler, Jarred Vanderbilt, Josie Canseco, Luka Sabbat, Michael Evans Behling, Asia Monet Ray, Indiana Massara, Stassie Karanikolaou, Suede Brooks, Erika Tham and Tylah Yaweh, while sipping cocktails and dancing the night away — it was truly a moment that we won’t forget!

Founded in 2014 by young power couple Paris Marchant and Lawrence Lee, Outcast’s mission was (and still is) to make a difference in the world of online shopping. From the day Outcast Clothing launched, the Marchant and Lee packed every parcel by hand from their one bedroom apartment. Each parcel represented an important, growing relationship with a customer. Today, Outcast Clothing has grown to become a go-to online destination for girls worldwide.

With a warehouse filled with the latest clothing, a passionate team who know exactly what you want from online shopping – convenience, fast shipping and most of all friendly service from a team that treats you like a friend!

You can shop the brand’s new arrivals on their website here, as well as follow them on Instagram at @_outcastclothing.

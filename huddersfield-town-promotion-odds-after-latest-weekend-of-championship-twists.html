Huddersfield Town promotion odds after latest weekend of Championship twists

Publish date: 2024-07-22

The bookmakers have updated their Championship promotion winning odds after the latest round of fixtures. The Terriers are one of the outsiders to win promotion to the Premier League despite their recent win over Hull City.

The win over the Tigers reignited Town’s promotion push after back to back defeats hampered the progression of Carlos Corberan’s side. Securing their play-off berth remains within their reach but are set to face promotion chasing rivals Middlesbrough and Luton Town in two of their remaining fixtures.

Sides around Town have games in hand and know the play-off picture could change completely in the course of the coming weeks. That’s reflected in the latest odds on oddschecker.

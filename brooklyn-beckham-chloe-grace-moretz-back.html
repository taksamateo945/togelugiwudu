Are Brooklyn Beckham and Chloe Grace Moretz Back On?

Publish date: 2024-07-25

Brooklyn Beckham and Chloe Grace Moretz have been pictured strolling down Shaftesbury Avenue in London on Friday night holding hands, which definitely confirms that the two lovebirds are back on!

The pair, as always, looked the part with Chloe, 19, sporting a statement leather jacket and Brooklyn, 17, dressed in a camouflage army print jacket and skinny jeans.

The 5th Wave star and Brooklyn have apparently revived their romance earlier this month as the two had dinner with Brooklyn’s parents, Victoria and David Beckham and Chloe’s mother Terri at Soho House in Los Angeles.

A source told British Newspaper ‘The Sun’ that they are ‘very much a couple’ and the pair were also ‘very comfortable getting their families together’. Victoria is said to have made an extensive effort to get to know Chloe better as well. The source said ‘She was sat chatting to her for ages and was clearly interested in finding out more about her life.’ Brooklyn and Chloe were also very comfortable together, with Chloe putting her head on Brooklyn’s shoulder continuously and Brooklyn doing the same.

Chloe has previously said in an interview that she isn’t ready for serious relationships at the moment as she wouldn’t be able to handle it. She said ‘I have no real plans. I don’t want anything right now. I can’t handle that right now.‘ However, Chloe might have changed her tune since then, and if she has we don’t blame her as it is clear the two have a special bond.

Throwing it back to 2014, they were first spotted together on several occasions in the summer but only as friends. They first met at Paris Fashion Week a few months before the summer of 2014 and during the summer they spent a lot of time together in LA, skateboarding and hanging around by the beach. They were also seen together at several music events, including Jay Z’s Made In America festival.

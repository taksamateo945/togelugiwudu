Their day at the park comes as as surprise, as only a few months ago, the social media influencer shared shocking images to Instagram following a 3 a.m. altercation at his $13M home on Oct. 11 where she was left bloodied and bruised. 

‘I didn’t show up “screaming” or uninvited. When I tried to leave he physically assaulted and refused to let me leave for hours,’ Swanson clarified via Instastory.

‘I’ve been emotionally, mentally, and physically abused and I’m not hiding it anymore…I’m so embarrassed and ashamed it had to get to this but I have to stand up for myself.’

‘I want everyone to know that the allegations against me are false. I was not arrested I took myself into the police station and cooperated,’ he wrote on his Instagram Story five days after the incident

A booking sheet obtained by DailyMail.com showed the Rack City singer had his bail set at $50,000 when he was arrested, but Tyga claimed he turned himself in to authorities when he finally spoke out against the allegations days later. 

One month after the incident, his case was transferred from the Los Angeles County District Attorney’s Office to the Los Angeles City Attorney’s Office after the DA chose not to pursue a felony charge.  

A rep for the Los Angeles City Attorney’s Office told ET in February, ‘Michael Ray Stevenson, a.k.a. Tyga, will not be charged with any criminal charges for his domestic violence case as long as he stays out of trouble legally.’ 

They added: ‘If not, prosecutors could end up filing misdemeanor charges.’

She’s currently in the throes of a legal battle with the Kardashian family after accusing them of influencing E! to cancel the reality show she starred in with Rob, which is expected to go to trial on April 15, one day after the premiere of their new Hulu show.

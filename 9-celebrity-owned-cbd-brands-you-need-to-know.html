    • More Celebrities in the CBD Business

    In 2021, the global cannabis market was worth $5.18 billion. We expect the industry to expand rapidly with more companies, including celebrity CBD brands, entering the sector. 

     These brands, owned by Hollywood stars, offer a range of products from CBD cannabis seeds to edibles to oils that help you relax. Have you tried some of these star-endorsed goodies?

     Here are the seven celebrity-owned CBD brands you need to know.

     1. Martha Stewart CBD

    Businesswoman Martha Stewart is America’s go-to lifestyle expert, offering guidance on everything from home decor to cooking. She also contributes considerably to the cannabis industry, producing innovative CBD products in collaboration with Canopy Growth Corporations.

    Her product line includes candies, tinctures, soft gels, topical creams, and pet treats. 

    The gummies come in different flavors, with the latest hitting the stores being the pumpkin spice. Thanks to the pure CBD isolate, gummies don’t produce intense highs but rather calming effects.   

    2. Game Up Nutrition

    The brand created by UFC fighters Nick and Nate Diaz focuses on delivering quality nutritional and wellness products. Game Up Nutrition uses the highest quality ingredients to produce CBD oils, edibles, topicals, and pet products.

    3. Happy Dance

    The last thing you’d expect from a celebrity brand is that it is average. That’s precisely what Kristen Bell has developed in Happy Dance.

    This celeb brand produces CBD skincare and beauty products. Some other products include CBD bath bombs and body butter. 

    4. Whoopi & Maya

    The legendary actress Whoopi Goldberg teamed up with Om Edibles owner Maya Elisabeth in 2016 to create Whoopi & Maya. The company specializes in CBD products that relieve menstrual pain and body aches. 

    Whoopi is a CBD enthusiast, endorsing body balms, bath products, tinctures, and edibles—all created by the company. 

    With products sold in over 300 stores across the country, Whoopi & Maya achieved immense success. Unfortunately, it was short-lived as the company closed in 2020.

    5. Willie’s Remedy

    Willie Nelson, a renowned country music artist, is among the celebrities who’re open about their marijuana use. As a long-standing cannabis advocate, he joined the booming CBD business trend with Willie’s Reserve. 

    The company focuses on THC products, selling branded weed strains, vape pens, and other accessories. In 2019, it launched a CBD product line called Willie’s Remedy.

    Under this brand, you’ll find hard edibles, topicals, and tinctures, as well as hemp oil-infused tea and coffee. Each product is made using first-grade ingredients, ensuring you enjoy all the terpenes of the cannabis plant.

    6. Etheridge Botanicals

    Melissa Etheridge is among celebrities who use CBD to enhance their health. She first used medical cannabis while battling breast cancer in 2004. Since then, she has advocated for the full legalization of cannabis. 

    The superstar singer owns Etheridge Botanicals, a California-based canna-business, which she started in 2020. It stocks flowers and pre-rolls, which can be shipped to over 30 states in the US.

    She’s in a partnership with Greenway Compassionate Relief which produces CBD-infused wine.

    7. 22red 

    Founded by System of a Down’s bassist, Shavo Odadjian, 22red is a CBD brand with modest offerings. The product list contains flowers, pre-rolls, CBD oils, resins, and CBD vapes. 

    The brand is committed to honoring cannabis plants and their link to creativity. As a life-long cannabis connoisseur, Shavo is on a mission to combine his passion with quality. 

    8. Tommy Chong CBD

    Comedian Tommy Chong has been a well-known advocate and activist for cannabis for four decades. So when he launched his CBD brand, it was no surprise. 

    Tommy Chong CBD boasts an excellent selection of products, including oils, gummies, topicals, and capsules. Not only are the products made with top-quality ingredients, but they’re also vegan and non-GMO. 

    9. Hartluck

    Motocross star Carey Kart created Hartluck, a CBD brand with an impressive selection of products. Among the typical oil and tincture, you’ll also find delicious gummies, topicals, and a CBD inhaler in the catalog. 

    All ingredients used in Hartluck products are sourced locally and of the highest quality. 

    The Booming Celeb CBD Industry

    Actors, musicians, footballers, and other famous individuals are jumping at the chance to be a part of the cannabis industry. Soon there’ll be various celebrity CBD brands. Get some of the products to experience all the benefits the plant offers.

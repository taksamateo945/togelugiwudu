Sarah Paulson glows in a white floral dress as she leads the stars at The Wrap'sPower Women Summit

Publish date: 2024-04-05

Sarah Paulson looked vibrant and stylish on Wednesday evening as she made an appearance at The Wrap’s 2021 Power Women Summit in West Hollywood.

The 46-year-old American Horror Story staple was joining other high-profile stars including Rosanna Arquette and model Alexis Ren for the event, which ‘aims to inspire and empower women’ in their professional careers and personal lives.

The acclaimed actress lit up the red carpet at The London West Hollywood at Beverly Hills hotel in a playful white dress decorated with minimalist black floral designs.

Standing out: Sarah Paulson, 46, was among the high-profile attendees of The Wrap’s 2021 Power Women Summit on Wednesday in West Hollywood. The event ‘aims to inspire and empower women’ in their professional careers and personal lives.

Sarah’s brilliant white dress reached down to her ankles and featured a pleated skirt.

She tightened the outfit around her midriff with a casual black Prada belt with a snapping buckle.

The Impeachment: American Crime Story star carried a sleek black clutch and wore an elegant pair of pointy black heels with an anklet.

She had her chic brunette bob partially tied back in a small bun, framing her face and exposing her sparkling diamond-encrusted earrings.
